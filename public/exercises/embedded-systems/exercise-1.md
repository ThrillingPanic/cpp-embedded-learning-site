# Exercise 1: Introduction to Embedded Systems

## Objective
The goal of this exercise is to familiarize yourself with the basic concepts of embedded systems and their applications.

## Instructions
1. **Research**: Read about embedded systems and their characteristics. Focus on the following aspects:
   - Definition of embedded systems
   - Common applications of embedded systems
   - Differences between embedded systems and general-purpose computers

2. **Questions**: Answer the following questions based on your research:
   - What are the key components of an embedded system?
   - How do embedded systems differ in terms of performance and power consumption compared to traditional computing systems?
   - Provide two examples of embedded systems in everyday life and explain their functions.

3. **Practical Task**: 
   - Choose a simple embedded system project (e.g., a temperature sensor or a basic LED control) and outline the steps you would take to design and implement it. Include:
     - Required components
     - Basic circuit diagram
     - Pseudocode for the main functionality

4. **Submission**: Compile your answers and project outline into a single document and submit it for review.
